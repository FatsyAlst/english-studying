# Academic — Reading

> 3 passages — 40 questions — 60 minutes

## Format

| Passage | Content | Source |
|---------|---------|-------|
| 1 | Shorter, accessible text | Magazines, newspapers |
| 2 | Medium-length, descriptive/analytical | Academic journals, books |
| 3 | Longer, complex, detailed | Academic/research content |

## Question Types

- Multiple choice
- Identifying information (True/False/Not Given)
- Identifying writer's views (Yes/No/Not Given)
- Matching information/headings/features/sentence endings
- Sentence/summary/note/table/flow-chart completion
- Diagram label completion
- Short-answer questions
