# Academic — Writing

> 2 tasks — 60 minutes total

## Format

| Task | Content | Time | Words |
|------|---------|------|-------|
| Task 1 | Describe/summarise visual information (graph, table, chart, diagram, map, process) | ~20 min | 150+ |
| Task 2 | Write an essay (opinion, discussion, problem-solution, advantages-disadvantages) | ~40 min | 250+ |

## Assessment Criteria

- Task achievement / Task response
- Coherence and cohesion
- Lexical resource
- Grammatical range and accuracy
