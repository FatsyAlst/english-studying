# Speaking

> 3 parts — 11-14 minutes total
> Same for both Academic and General Training
> Conducted face-to-face (or via video for IELTS Online)

## Format

| Part | Content | Duration |
|------|---------|----------|
| 1 | Introduction & general questions (familiar topics) | 4-5 min |
| 2 | Individual long turn (cue card topic — 1 min prep, 1-2 min talk) | 3-4 min |
| 3 | Two-way discussion (abstract topics related to Part 2) | 4-5 min |

## Assessment Criteria

- Fluency and coherence
- Lexical resource (vocabulary)
- Grammatical range and accuracy
- Pronunciation
