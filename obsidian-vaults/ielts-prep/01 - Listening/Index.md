# Listening

> 4 sections — 40 questions — 30 minutes (+10 min transfer time on paper-based)
> Same for both Academic and General Training

## Format

| Section | Content | Questions |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| 1 | Conversation between 2 people (everyday context) | 10 |
| 2 | Monologue (everyday context) | 10 |
| 3 | Conversation between 2-4 people (educational/training context) | 10 |
| 4 | Academic monologue (lecture) | 10 |

## Question Types

- Multiple choice
- Matching
- Plan/map/diagram labelling
- Form/note/table/flow-chart/summary completion
- Sentence completion
