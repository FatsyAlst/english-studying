## ğŸ’¡ Usage Notes
> [!tip] Notes
> - **Embargo vs boycott:** embargo = government-imposed (official policy); boycott = voluntary refusal by individuals/groups.
> - **Embargo vs sanction:** embargo = specific type focusing on trade; sanction = broader term (freezing assets, travel bans, etc.).
> - Common mistake: plural is **embargoes** (not "embargos").
> - Formal register; common in political, diplomatic, and media contexts.
