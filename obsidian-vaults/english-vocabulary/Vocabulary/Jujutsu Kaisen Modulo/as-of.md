> [!tip] Notes
> - Two main uses: **snapshot/status** ("As of March 14th, my portfolio was worth...") and **effective date** ("As of today, new rules apply").
> - "As of" vs "on": "As of Monday, we completed three tasks" = done on or before Monday; "On Monday" = specifically that day.
