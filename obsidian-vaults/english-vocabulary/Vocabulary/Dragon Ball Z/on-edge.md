---
> [!tip] Notes
> - Informal; describes temporary tension or anxiety.
> - Often used to describe how someone feels before an important event or while under stress.
> - Can be used as a predicate adjective: 'She is on edge.' or 'I feel on edge.'
> - Related idiom: 'on the edge of one's seat' (excited and tense).
