---
> [!tip] Notes
> - Formal when describing gathering people; neutral for building/constructing.
> - Can be transitive (assemble something) or intransitive (assemble at a place).
> - Common in manufacturing and construction contexts.
> - 'Assembly' is the noun form; 'assembler' refers to a person or machine that assembles.
