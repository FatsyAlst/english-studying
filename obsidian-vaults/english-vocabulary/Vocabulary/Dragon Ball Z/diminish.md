---
> [!tip] Notes
> - Formal; often suggests gradual reduction.
> - Can be intransitive (something diminishes) or transitive (diminish something).
> - Related noun: diminution (act of diminishing).
> - Common phrase: 'diminishing returns' (decreasing benefit from continued effort).
