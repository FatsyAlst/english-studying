---
> [!tip] Notes
> - Noun dominant in modern use, especially in safety/health contexts.
> - Verb form is more literary and formal.
> - The adjective 'hazardous' is far more common than the verb form.
> - Differentiate from 'hassle' (annoyance or difficulty).
