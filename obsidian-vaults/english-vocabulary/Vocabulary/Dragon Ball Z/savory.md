> [!tip] Notes
> - Culinary term: opposite of sweet (salty, spicy, umami).
> - Examples: pizza, cheese, meat, olives = savory; cake, chocolate = sweet.
> - Spelling: AmE 'savory', BrE 'savoury'.
> - Not to be confused with 'savory' the herb (Satureja hortensis).
