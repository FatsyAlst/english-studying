Suggesting that something bad, unpleasant, or threatening is likely to happen; foreboding, portentous, or inauspicious.

---
- Ominous clouds/silence/rumble/threat
- Ominous sign/portent
- Ominous note/tone
- Grow ominous
- Loom ominously

---
Late 16th century from Latin ominosus 'full of foreboding,' from omen, omin- 'omen/portent.' Originally neutral for any omen; now mostly negative.

---
> [!tip] Notes
> - Evokes dread/forewarning; stronger than 'threatening.' Synonyms: foreboding, portentous, menacing.
> - No major BrE/AmE differences.
