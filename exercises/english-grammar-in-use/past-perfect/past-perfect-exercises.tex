\section*{Instructions}

This practice set focuses on the \textbf{Past Perfect} tense:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Form: \textbf{had + past participle} (had done, had seen, had gone, etc.)
    \item Use: To talk about something that happened \textbf{before} another past action or time
    \item Key point: The past perfect shows which action happened \textbf{first} in the past
\end{itemize}
